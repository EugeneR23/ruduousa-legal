Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. Whose data is processed

RU DUO USA Post Center is a personal desktop tool used only by its owners. It does not collect, store, or process any data from third-party users, because it has no third-party users.

2. What data the tool handles

  • Our own video files — highlight clips created from our own streams, stored locally on our own computer.
  • Our own account credentials — OAuth tokens for our own social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, etc.), stored locally on our own computer and never transmitted anywhere except to the respective platform's official API.
  • Our own publication metadata — titles, descriptions, and view statistics of our own published videos, stored locally.

3. What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect any personal data from viewers or any other people.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.
  • We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics on this site.
  • We do not access any TikTok user data other than our own account, as authorized through TikTok's official OAuth flow.

4. Data retention and deletion

All data lives on the owners' local computer and can be deleted by the owners at any time. Platform access can be revoked at any time through each platform's security settings (for TikTok: Settings → Security → Apps).
